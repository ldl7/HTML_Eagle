Advanced Weaponry: The F-15 is equipped with a diverse array of weapons including air-to-air missiles like the AIM-120 AMRAAM and AIM-9 Sidewinder, as well as air-to-ground munitions such as JDAMs and AGM-88 HARM missiles, enabling it to engage both aerial and ground targets with precision.
Cutting-Edge Radar Systems: The F-15 features sophisticated radar systems such as the AN/APG-63(V)3 Active Electronically Scanned Array (AESA) radar, offering enhanced detection and tracking capabilities, improved reliability, and reduced vulnerability to electronic countermeasures Electronic Warfare Suites: Equipped with advanced electronic warfare suites like the AN/ALQ-131 and AN/ALQ-135, the F-15 can detect, jam, and counter enemy radar and communication systems, enhancing its survivability in hostile environments. Stealth Features: Although not designed as a stealth aircraft, the F-15 has undergone upgrades to incorporate stealth features such as radar-absorbent materials and reduced radar cross-section enhancements, improving its survivability against radar detection. Countermeasures and Defensive Systems: The F-15 is equipped with a variety of countermeasures and defensive systems, including chaff and flare dispensers, radar warning receivers (RWRs), and electronic warfare (EW) systems, to detect and evade incoming threats such as missiles and enemy aircraft.

Summary:

  • Air-to-Air Missiles: AIM-120 Advanced Medium-Range Air-to-Air Missile (AMRAAM) AIM-9 Sidewinder
  • Air-to-Ground Munitions: Joint Direct Attack Munition (JDAM) AGM-65 Maverick
  • Precision-Guided Munitions: AGM-154 Joint Standoff Weapon (JSOW) GBU-39 Small Diameter Bomb (SDB)
  • Multi-Role Missions: AGM-88 High-Speed Anti-Radiation Missile (HARM) AGM-158 Joint Air-to-Surface Standoff Missile (JASSM)
